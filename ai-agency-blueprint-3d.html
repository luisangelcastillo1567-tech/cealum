LESSON 4
Your Offer Stack

An offer stack is a psychological architecture — designed to make "yes" easy and "no" feel like a loss. Three tiers, three entry points.

TIER 1 · ENTRY
$297 – $497

Single deliverable, fast turnaround. Chatbot, content calendar, or AI audit. Goal: get them to experience your work.

TIER 2 · CORE
$1,000 – $3,000

Comprehensive solution — full AI system build. Setup, training, 30 days support. Where real revenue lives.

TIER 3 · RETAINER
$500 – $3,000/month

Ongoing management and optimization. 5 clients × $1K/mo = $60K/year recurring. This is wealth.

THE NAMING TRICK
Don't sell "AI chatbot setup." Sell the "Sovereign Response System." A name implies methodology. Methodology implies expertise. Expertise justifies premium pricing.
LESSON 5
Client Acquisition Machine

Channel 2 · LinkedIn DMs

Channel 3 · Content Flywheel

Outreach = short-term clients. Content = long-term authority. Each post teaches something and positions you as the expert.

LESSON 9
Cold Outreach Scripts

Tested across hundreds of messages. Copy. Customize. Send.

Script 1 · The Pattern Interrupt
cold-email-v1.txt
Subject: Quick question about [Business Name] Hi [First Name], I was looking at [Business Name]'s website and noticed you don't have an AI chatbot handling customer questions. With your inquiry volume, that's probably costing your team 15-20 hours/month in repetitive responses. I build AI chat systems for [industry] businesses that handle 80%+ of questions instantly — 24/7, no extra staff. My last client cut response time from 4 hours to 12 seconds. Would it make sense for me to show you a quick 10-min demo? Best, [Your Name]
LESSON 9 · CONTINUED
Script 2 · LinkedIn DM
linkedin-dm.txt
Hey [First Name] — noticed your [business type] has been growing. Congrats on the expansion. Quick thought: I've been helping similar businesses automate their [process] using AI. Just saved a [similar biz] about 25 hours/month. Would you be open to seeing how it works? No pitch, just a quick look.
Script 3 · Follow-Up (Day 3)
follow-up-day3.txt
Hi [First Name] — floating this back up in case it got buried. I put together a 2-min video showing what an AI system could look like for [Business Name]. Happy to send it over if you're curious. Either way, keep up the great work with [specific thing about their business]. [Your Name]
KEY PRINCIPLES
Always reference something specific. Lead with results. Under 100 words. Low-commitment CTA. Follow up 3x minimum — most replies come on follow-up 2 or 3.
